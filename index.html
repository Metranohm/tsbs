About TSBS

Built on a foundation of community, respect and honesty, TSBS is a corporation of experienced school Medicaid consultants. Medicaid billing for the state-wide School Health and Related Services (SHARS) program helps to increase many school districts' fiscal income. Our timely, accurate and service-oriented team looks forward to working with you.
